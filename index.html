Company Overview +

Arch Street Capital Advisors "Arch Street" is a minority-owned, full-service real estate investment firm. Founded in 2003, with offices in New York and London, Arch Street has completed more than $9.7 billion in U.S. real estate transactions across major and niche commercial property sectors. Since inception, Arch Street has invested capital for a client base of institutional investors, exclusively on a separate account basis. Arch Business Critical Income Fund I ("ABC Income Fund I") will represent the first opportunity for institutional investors to participate as limited partners in a closed-end fund vehicle managed by Arch Street as general partner.

  • Senior Management Team Averages 20+ Years of Real Estate Experience and 16+ Years Working Together
  • 80% Diverse Senior Management Team; 50%+ of Overall Team is Comprised of Women or Minorities
  • 20+ Years investing in Business-Critical, Single-Tenant, Long-term Leased Assets (the “Investment Strategy”)
